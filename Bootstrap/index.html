About Us

Navi Dish Swam Foundation is a non-profit organization working to bring meaningful change in the lives of the most underprivileged members of society. We believe that every individual deserves access to education, healthcare, hygiene, and livelihood opportunities—regardless of their identity or circumstances.

We run several projects including education and mentorship for children, health camps in rural areas, plantation drives for the environment, and skill development programs to support employment.

Our goal is to create a society where every person feels proud of who they are and can move towards a brighter future. Let's come together and ignite a new light of hope.
